Check if documents can be legalised

You can get a UK public document ‘legalised’ - this means a signature, seal or stamp made by a UK public official on the document is confirmed as genuine by the UK government.

Use this page to check if your document can be legalised.

For example, to find Birth Certificate click on the 'General Registry Office Documents' box and then click on 'Birth Certificate' for information about legalising the document.
