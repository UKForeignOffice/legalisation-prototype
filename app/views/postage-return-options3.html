Returning your documents

Select a delivery method for the return of your documents

Stamped self-addressed envelope instructions

  1. Add stamps on to an A4-sized envelope - enough to cover posting your documents back:
    • the correct amount depends on the size, weight and delivery preferences - returning 1 document will cost approximately £1 to £2
    • we recommend you go to your local post office who will help you buy the right postage
  2. Write your return address on the envelope - we'll use this envelope to send your documents back to you when they've been legalised.
  3. Put this stamped self-addressed envelope together with your documents in a separate envelope addressed to the Legalisation Office. It's ok to fold it if it doesnt fit.
