Returning your documents

Select a delivery method for the return of your documents
Your documents will be couriered in a waterproof envelope. If you want additional protection, when you send us your application include an envelope which will hold your documents unfolded (typically A4 size or larger)

Stamped self-addressed envelope instructions

  1. Add stamps on to a large envelope - enough to cover posting your documents back:
    • we recommend you go to your local post office who will help you buy the right postage
    • the correct amount depends on the size, weight and delivery preferences - returning 1 document will cost approximately £1 to £2
    • use an envelope which will hold your documents unfolded - A4 or larger, classified as a 'Large Letter' by Royal Mail
  2. Write your return address on the envelope - we'll use this envelope to send your documents back to you when they've been legalised.
  3. Put this stamped self-addressed envelope together with your documents in a separate envelope addressed to the Legalisation Office.
